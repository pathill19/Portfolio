Gig

Overview

Gig is a hyper local music finding app based in Brooklyn, NY. Our team collaboratively created personas, researched competitors, created paper prototypes, conducted user research and finally created a high fidelity prototype.

Personas

We mocked up two of our primary users, someone seeking to use our app to find a gig to attend, and a band member who will use our app to promote their gig.

Paper Prototypes and User Testing

Based on our value proposition, user research, competitive and comparative research, we designed a storyboard to create a guessable app which allows our users to find live nearby gigs, offered at all times, in Brooklyn seamlessly and quickly. Our friends Leslie Koo, Amy Ganz, and Patrick Warren helped test our usability by trying to navigate through our app as they fulfill a list of tasks we provided for them.
